Fight VR!

At 2016 SD-Hacks, a friend and I developed, tested, and delivered a virtual reality throwback to the classic Nintendo gem, Mike Tyson's Punch Out! We used the Unity Engine and the HTC Vive for the game itself along with an Arduino with vibration motors to simulate the feeling of hit / being hit.

Outside Recs

At the 2015 Outside Lands hackathon, I developed a recommendation platform for attendees of the Bay Area festival, Outside Lands. Using Spotify API, the app would analyze the user's music preferences to compile a schedule of artists that the user would most enjoy.

  • Check it out!

QuestList

QuestList is a Django-powered web application that recommends jobs nearby that need quick fixes, such as painting a house or mowing a lawn. Users can act as both a supplier, posting jobs that need temporary help, and as a consumer, completing these tasks and making a quick buck!
