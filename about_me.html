About Me

Howdy! I'm Haaris Khan, a CS major at UC Berkeley. I come from the chicken-loving town of Petaluma, and moved ALL the way to Berkeley in 2015.

I'm interested in a multitude of things, in a multitude of subjects, but my core interests in my field are Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning, and Virtual / Augmented Reality (though I'm still exploring these interests).

Outside of academics, I'm very interested in entrepreneurship. I was influenced by my great uncle, who was an arguably grey role model for me growing up (he was great for life-skills like instilling perseverance and confidence, but terrible for his unhealthy eating habits and minimal sleeping habits).

For fun, I like to read novels (Malcolm Gladwell is godly), program random but useful widgets, and workout / run intensely!
